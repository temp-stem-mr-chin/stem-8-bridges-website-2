How Was The Bridge Tested?

We used a bridge crusher. It pressed down on the top of the bridge and used 4 force crushers to measure in pounds how much it weighed. Our score was measured by our mass held in kilograms / mass of our bridge in kilograms.

The Results

Our brdige held 0.454 kilograms! Although that was the lowest mass held out of any group, we had the lowest weight of any bridge, 0.028 kilograms. Our score was 16.2! We got 3rd place!
